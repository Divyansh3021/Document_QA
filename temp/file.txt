Angela Gabrielle White is an Australian pornographic film actress and director.[2] She has been inducted into the AVN Hall of Fame and the XRCO Hall of Fame, and in 2020 became AVN's first three-time Female Performer of the Year winner.[3][1]

Education
White graduated from the University of Melbourne in 2010[4] with a first-class honours degree in gender studies.[2] There she researched women's experiences in the Australian pornography industry. White's honours thesis, "The Porn Performer: The Radical Potential of Pleasure in Pornography", was published in The Routledge Companion to Media, Sex and Sexuality in 2017.[5]

In 2014, she said that she would like to go back to university to complete her Ph.D. and expand her studies to include a broader cross-section of performers.[6]

Pornography career
White began her career in the adult industry in 2003 via adult modelling at the age of 18.[7][8] Her first scene was shot by Score, also at the age of 18.[7] Her debut hardcore pornographic DVD was released in 2011.[8]

XBIZ has called White "Australia's most well-known adult performer",[9] and The Daily Beast has called her "The Meryl Streep of Porn."[3] In 2013, White launched her official website, angelawhite.com.[10]

In October 2014, White signed a distribution deal with Girlfriends Films to release films.[11] In the same month, Fleshlight, makers of a masturbation tool for men, named White their newest Fleshlight Girl.[12] She is the first Australian to be named a Fleshlight Girl.[13]

In September 2016, in an interview with XBIZ, White said she had moved to the United States and signed with Spiegler Girls to shoot for companies like Brazzers, Naughty America, and Jules Jordan.[14]

In January 2018, White co-hosted the 35th annual AVN Awards Show in Las Vegas, Nevada, alongside comedian Aries Spears and webcam star Harli Lotts.[15]

In October 2021, White signed an exclusive contract with Brazzers. Brazzers producer Ryan Hogan said at the time White represented "the absolute peak of professionalism and stardom in our industry. Everybody knows Angela White."[16]

In July 2022, Brazzers released "Sexually Rated Programming", a short starring White "set in a dystopian world that sees Angela free a submissive society brainwashed by anti-erotic propaganda." The scene features a blowbang.[17]

White has also done regular work as a webcam model.[18][7]
